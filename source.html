How to play

This game is called Hangman. It is a game that is played in school to this day. To beat this game you have to fill in the underlined spots by draging the letters on green spot. If you guess the right letter , it will fill all underlined spots where this letter should be ,but if you guess wrong the pictures of Hangman will begin to show. If you guess the word right you will get a point. There are 10 levels in total to beat. Can you beat them all?
